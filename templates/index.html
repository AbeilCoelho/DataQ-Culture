Data Quality Assessment for Cultural Heritage.

DataQ Culture is an innovative data quality tool for cultural heritage collections. Developed as a master's project at the Federal University of Esp√≠rito Santo (Ufes) in Brazil, it helps professionals and institutions ensure the integrity and accuracy of their data. Learn more in this article (In Brazilian portuguese).

Based on the international Catalogue of Cultural Objects (CCO) standard, DataQ Culture calculates a quality index reflecting confidence in data integrity and accuracy. This index uses CCO guidelines.

DataQ Culture offers a simple, efficient solution for ensuring data quality in cultural heritage, enabling the identification and correction of errors before they cause damage. It also allows real-time monitoring to ensure data remains accurate and up-to-date with CCO standards.
